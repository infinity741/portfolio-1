Smart Irrigation System

Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL, MySQL, PHP, Laravel and Blade.

So, We know that there is scarcity of fresh water for our future generations and hence we need to make our water use regulated so that we don't waste this natural resource. Much water is used in irrigation just to water the crops and somwtimes farmers just keep the water on, leading to water wastage and soil degradation too.

Hence, We've developed a solution for that and it is much more than just water regulator system. It helps the farmers in many unexpected ways. We've also included soil monitoring which is fully automated process and farmer can a full report just on his smartphone.

This system checks the moisture content of the soil, whether the soil needs water or not and how much of water it will be using to make soil moist upto a certaion level suitable for the crop and according to this data, the water supply is turned off and on automatically. Also, there is an application developed from where we can turn on the motor just be tapping a switch in app.

This application also provides soil data to farmer through the app. It tells the NPK levels of the soil, any cattle presence on farm and it also does the rain checks and tells the probability of rain and keeps the water supply off if it is raining. In future, some features like telling what fertilisers to use with which type of crops will be told to the farmers.
