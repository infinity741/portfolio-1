Two-Wheeler Locking System

  • Tech Stack: Arduino Uno, GPS module, GSM module.

    • This Project was made in consideration of Two Wheeler Theft that are happening so frequently nowadays. So, to minimise those mishaps, we've developed a smart lock that will make sure that the vehicle is protected.

    Whenever a thief tries to steal the bike or any vehicle and when he will sit on it, an alarm will be trigerred and the owner will get a SMS on his device that someone has taken his bike and when ignition is turned on, the owner will then get the live location of the bike in the form of the co-ordinates.

    This system is fully automated and can be turned on and off according to the need of the user/vehicle owner.

    This system has been tested on a real life vehicle and it worked well and is ready to be implemented on any two wheeler.
